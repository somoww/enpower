About the Project

Most homes with solar panels generate more electricity than they use. This extra energy is often wasted or sold at low prices.

Our Solution:
We store the extra energy and use AI to analyze electricity price trends, just like the stock market. Then, we sell it at the best possible time for maximum profit.

How It Works

  • Connect your smart meter and storage system to our platform.
  • Our AI tracks electricity prices in real-time.
  • When prices peak, we automatically sell your stored energy.
  • You earn more, while using clean energy smarter.

Key Features

  • Real-time energy tracking
  • Smart AI-based price prediction
  • Automated electricity selling
  • User dashboard with stats
  • Secure and easy to use
